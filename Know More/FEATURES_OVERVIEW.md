# Features Overview

Sushruta provides a rich set of features tailored to the needs of seniors and their caregivers.

## 1. Dashboard
*   **Central Hub**: A unified view of the day's tasks, vitals, and upcoming events.
*   **Quick Actions**: Easy access to emergency contacts and daily routines.
*   **Vitals Summary**: Real-time display of heart rate, blood pressure, and steps.

## 2. Medicine Management
*   **Routine Tracking**: Logs daily medications with dosage and timing instructions (e.g., "After Breakfast").
*   **Reminders**: Visual indicators for taken/missed doses.
*   **Inventory**: Tracks stock levels and expiry dates.

## 3. Profile & Personal Info
*   **Identity**: Stores essential personal details (Name, DOB, Blood Group).
*   **Emergency Contacts**: Primary and secondary contacts (Guardians, Doctors) for quick access.
*   **Medical History**: Logs allergies, chronic conditions, and past surgeries.

## 4. Digital Health Records
*   **Insurance Vault**: Stores insurance policy details for easy retrieval.
*   **Reports Archive**: upload and organize lab reports and prescriptions.
*   **Accessible Data**: Eliminates the need for physical paper files.

## 5. GPS & Safety
*   **Location Tracking**: (Conceptual/Planned) Allows caretakers to monitor the senior's location.
*   **Geofencing**: Alerts if the senior leaves a designated safe area.

## 6. Wellness Module
*   **Physical Health**: Tracks steps, sleep patterns, and exercise logs.
*   **Emotional Well-being**: "Joy" tab featuring curated content (videos, music) to uplift mood.
*   **Holistic Approach**: Encourages a balanced lifestyle beyond just medication.

## 7. Appointment Scheduling
*   **Doctor Connect**: Manage appointments with healthcare providers.
*   **Reminders**: Notifications for upcoming visits.
*   **History**: Logs past consultations.

## 8. Government Schemes
*   **Information Portal**: Provides details on relevant government health schemes and benefits for seniors.
*   **Access**: Simplifies finding financial and medical aid.

## 9. AI Assistant
*   **Virtual Helper**: An AI-powered chatbot to answer health queries and assist with app navigation.

## 10. Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)
*   **Senior View**: Simplified, large-text interface focused on daily tasks.
*   **Caretaker View**: Dashboard to monitor the senior's adherence and vitals remotely.
*   **Doctor View**: Clinical dashboard to review patient history and reports.
